## Reordering variables and trying again:

Regsubsets (cont.)

  • \(C_p\) and BIC rates are various error terms used to analyze subset model selection
  • Looking to maximize the adjusted R-squared value, and minimize the other two error rates
  • Optimal variable selection: 3-5 variables

Regression Analysis

newFit = glm(ruff_turnout~Under5 + FiveTo17 + 
             FortyFiveTo54 + One_9999_loss,
             data = trainingData)
  • Our model can be depicted as:

\[Y = -0.92 + 10.13X_1 - 3.39X_2 + 9.32X_3 + 2.28X_4\]

Where:

  • \(Y\) = Voter turnout
  • \(X_1\) = Percentage of district population under the age of five
  • \(X_2\) = Percentage of district population within the ages of five to seventeen
  • \(X_3\) = Percentage of district population within the ages of forty-five to fifty-four
  • \(X_4\) = Percentage of distrcit population with an annual income of $20,000
